About Me

Welcome, My name is Alexey Geht and I am a certified Yoga Teacher. I have practiced yoga since 2015, training under esteemed teachers Mukesh Kothari in Budapest and Roxanne in Israel. My journey with yoga has been transformative, and I am passionate about sharing its benefits with others. Join my classes every Monday at 9 a.m. at Body and Mind Studio in Vác, where we explore Ashtanga Vinyasa Yoga together.
